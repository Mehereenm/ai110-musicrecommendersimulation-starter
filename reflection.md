# Reflection: Profile Comparisons 

**Pair 1: Pop vs. Lofi**
Completely different results, as expected. Energy separates them clearly (high vs. low). This works well.

**Pair 2: Pop vs. Rock**
Different at the top (genre matters), but start to look similar later due to limited songs. High energy causes overlap.

**Pair 3: Rock vs. Moody Metal**
Almost identical results. Since “sad” doesn’t exist, that preference is ignored, so ranking is mostly by energy.

**Pair 4: Unknown Genre vs. Lofi**
Similar taste, but unknown genre scores lower just because it’s missing from the data. Genre acts like a gate.

**Pair 5: Pop vs. Contradictory**
Almost no change. Acoustic preference is too weak, so energy dominates.

**Takeaway:**
Works when data and preferences align. Fails with small datasets, missing genres, or conflicting preferences—and never warns the user.
